A Message from the Bishopric

Umlayezo ovela kubabhishophi
From Bishop Thomas Moeti Mahlo:

"Dear Brothers and Sisters of Soweto Ward, Sanibonani. As we begin this new month, let us focus on strengthening our personal relationships with the Savior. February is a month of love, and what better way to show love than by following Christ's example in our daily interactions? Let us make time for meaningful prayer, scripture study, and service. My counselors and I pray for each of you and your families. Ngiyanithanda nonke."

2026 Study Focus: Old Testament

This Month's "Come, Follow Me" Focus

2026 Study Focus: Old Testament

"All things which have been given of God from the beginning of the world... are the typifying of Christ" (2 Nephi 11:4)

"The Foundations of Faith"
February: Genesis 5-9; Moses 6-7
Weekly Breakdown (Old Testament 2026):
  • Week 1 (Feb 1-7): Genesis 5; Moses 6 - The Generations of Adam and the Preaching of Enoch
  • Week 2 (Feb 8-14): Moses 7 - Enoch's Vision and the City of Zion
  • Week 3 (Feb 15-21): Genesis 6-7; Moses 8 - Noah and the Flood
  • Week 4 (Feb 22-28): Genesis 8-9; Moses 7:62-69 - The Covenant of the Rainbow
Monthly Invitation: Read about Enoch's faith in Moses 6-7 and identify one principle you can apply to build your own "city of Zion" in your home.
Family Challenge: Read the story of Noah (Genesis 6-9) together and discuss what modern "arks" we need to build to protect our families spiritually.
Sunday School Connection: Our Sunday School lessons on 1st & 3rd Sundays will deepen our study of these Old Testament chapters.
